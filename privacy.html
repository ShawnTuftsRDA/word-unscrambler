Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2025  |  Effective: April 18, 2025

🔒 The short version: QuickTools does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Everything runs in your browser. We are a free tool — not a data business.

1. Who We Are

QuickTools ("we", "us", "our") is a collection of free online utility tools operated by ParaRigDynamics LLC, a service-disabled veteran-owned small business based in St. Johns County, Florida, USA. Our tools are available at quicktools.io and related subdomains.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Account information (no accounts required)
  • Payment information
  • The content you enter into our tools (word unscrambler inputs, calculator values, etc.)
  • Location data beyond the general country level (from analytics)

3. Information That Is Collected Automatically

Like most websites, we use the following standard web technologies:

3a. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our tools. This collects anonymous data including pages visited, time spent, device type, and general geographic region (country/region). This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

3b. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our tools. AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history. This is controlled entirely by Google. For information on how Google uses data when you use our sites, see Google's Privacy & Terms. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ad Settings.

3c. Browser Local Storage

Some of our tools (such as the Joke Generator's saved favorites feature) store data locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

4. Cookies

QuickTools itself does not set cookies. However, Google Analytics and Google AdSense may set cookies on our pages. These are third-party cookies governed by Google's privacy policy. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

5. Children's Privacy

Our tools are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Third-Party Links

Some of our tools may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is minimal security risk to your personal information. All our tools run entirely in your browser — nothing you type is transmitted to our servers.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are a resident of the European Union (GDPR) or California (CCPA), you have rights regarding your personal data. Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to access, delete, or correct. For data collected by Google (Analytics/AdSense), please refer to Google's privacy controls.

For California residents: We do not sell personal information. We do not have actual knowledge that we sell personal information of consumers under 16 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our tools after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

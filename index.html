Frequently Asked Questions

How does the word unscrambler work?
Our tool checks every possible combination of your letters against a dictionary of over 170,000 valid English words. It returns all matches instantly, sorted by length so you can find the best plays quickly.
Can I use this for Wordle?
Absolutely. Enter the letters you know and unscramble them to find 5-letter words. Use the length filter to show only 5-letter results.
Does it work for Scrabble and Words with Friends?
Yes. Our dictionary includes words valid in both Scrabble and Words with Friends. The Scrabble score shown is based on standard tile values.
What is the maximum number of letters?
You can enter up to 15 letters. Most word games use 7-10 letter racks, so this covers all common use cases.
